Precious Materials

We source only the finest materials from around the world. Our gold is ethically sourced from Swiss refineries, our diamonds are D-Flawless grade, and our leather straps are crafted from alligator hides tanned using centuries-old techniques.

18K Gold & Platinum

Hand-polished to a mirror finish that reflects light with unparalleled brilliance.

Exotic Materials

From meteorite dials to ceramic bezels, each material is selected for its unique properties.

In-House Movement

Every LuxChronos timepiece is powered by a completely in-house designed and manufactured movement. Our Calibre LC-01 features a double-barrel with 80 hours of power reserve and is decorated by hand with Côtes de Genève.

Swiss Precision

Each movement undergoes rigorous testing at the Contrôle Officiel Suisse des Chronomètres (COSC), ensuring accuracy within -4/+6 seconds per day. Our tourbillons achieve even greater precision, within ±1 second per day.

Artisan Finishing

Every component receives hand-finishing: beveled edges, polished screws, and Geneva stripes applied by master craftsmen. The result is not just a timepiece, but a wearable work of art.
